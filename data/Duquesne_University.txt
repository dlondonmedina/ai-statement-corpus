Duquesne University official policy on generative AI Artificial Intelligence
https://www.duq.edu/documents/academics/colleges-and-schools/law/policies-and-procedures-ay2023-2024.pdf
Submitting work for academic credit, or in fulfillment of requirements where which utilized
Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools, including but not limited to ChatGPT, unless
such use is specifically permitted by your professor. Further note, information derived from
Generative AI tools is based on other published material found on the Internet and therefore
using such material without citation, even when permitted, may rise to the level of Plagiarism.
