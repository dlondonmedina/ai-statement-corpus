The emergence of ChatGPT and other similar Generative AI platforms is certainly impacting on education – in particular, on assessments. 
UNSW academics and staff have been paving the way by creating useful resources and thought-leadership articles. These resources help HE educators navigate the integration of Artificial Intelligence within the classroom and ensure the continuation of effective learning and teaching.
